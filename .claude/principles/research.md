## Research Guidelines

- Read widely before concluding. One file is never enough — trace the data flow through all layers end-to-end.
- Find ALL consumers of the thing you're researching. Use grep/glob to search the entire codebase, not just the directory you're in.
- Understand the existing patterns before suggesting changes. How is similar functionality already handled?
- Present findings, not solutions. During research, your job is to understand and report — not to jump to implementation.
</research-principles>
